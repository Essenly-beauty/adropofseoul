## What it is

Heartleaf extract comes from Houttuynia cordata, known in Korea as eoseongcho.
It became a modern K-beauty favorite through watery toners and ampoules aimed
at skin that is both oily and easily irritated.

## What it does

It is used for calming visible redness and making breakout-prone skin feel less
angry without adding heavy moisture. Many formulas position it as a daily
support ingredient rather than a harsh acne treatment.

## How to use it

Use it after cleansing as a toner or serum step. It fits well before lightweight
moisturizers, sunscreen, and barrier creams.
