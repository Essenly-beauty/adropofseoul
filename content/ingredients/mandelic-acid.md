## What it is

Mandelic acid is an alpha hydroxy acid derived from bitter almond chemistry.
Its larger molecular size means it tends to work more gradually on the skin
surface than glycolic acid.

## What it does

It helps with uneven texture, dullness, and post-blemish marks while often
feeling more manageable for sensitive or breakout-prone skin. It is a smart
choice when you want polish without a dramatic peel feeling.

## How to use it

Use it at night a few times a week. Pair with calming hydration and sunscreen,
and pause if your skin becomes shiny-tight, hot, or flaky.
