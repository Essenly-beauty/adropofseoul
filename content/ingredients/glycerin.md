## What it is

Glycerin is one of skincare's most reliable humectants. It is not trendy or
flashy, but it appears everywhere: cleansers, toners, essences, creams, masks,
and sunscreens.

## What it does

It helps draw and hold water in the skin's outer layer, supporting a smoother,
more comfortable barrier. Because it is compatible with so many ingredients,
it often acts as the quiet hydration base underneath more famous actives.

## How to use it

You do not need a dedicated glycerin serum. Look for it in formulas where you
want comfort: low-pH cleansers, barrier creams, hydrating toners, and daily
moisturizers.
