## What it is

Panthenol is a pro-vitamin form of vitamin B5. In Korean skincare it often
appears in "barrier," "cica," and "derma" formulas because it hydrates without
feeling aggressive.

## What it does

It acts as a humectant while also helping skin feel calmer and more resilient.
That makes it useful after over-cleansing, exfoliation, retinoids, cold
weather, or any stretch where the skin barrier feels thin and fussy.

## How to use it

Use panthenol in a toner, serum, cream, or sleeping mask. It pairs beautifully
with ceramides, glycerin, centella, madecassoside, and niacinamide.
