## What it is

Ceramide NP is a type of ceramide, one of the lipids naturally found in the
skin barrier. In skincare it is usually paired with cholesterol, fatty acids,
panthenol, or other moisturizers to help mimic the skin's own protective layer.

## What it does

When the barrier is depleted, skin can feel tight, rough, red, and reactive.
Ceramide NP helps replenish the lipid environment that keeps water in and
irritants out, making it a natural fit for Korean "barrier repair" creams.

## How to use it

Use it in the moisturizer step, especially at night or after active ingredients.
It is helpful in winter, after travel, or during a retinoid routine.
