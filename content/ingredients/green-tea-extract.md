## What it is

Green tea extract is made from Camellia sinensis leaves. It is a natural fit for
Korean skincare because it feels familiar, light, and versatile across toners,
serums, creams, and masks.

## What it does

It brings antioxidant support while helping skin look calmer and less shiny.
That makes it useful for routines built around daily protection rather than a
single dramatic active.

## How to use it

Use green tea in morning antioxidant layers, lightweight moisturizers, or
soothing masks. It pairs well with sunscreen, niacinamide, heartleaf, and
panthenol.
