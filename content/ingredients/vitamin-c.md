## What it is

Vitamin C is a family of brightening antioxidant ingredients. Pure ascorbic acid
is the most famous, while Korean products also use gentler derivatives in
toners, ampoules, creams, and masks.

## What it does

It helps skin look brighter and more even while adding antioxidant support
against daily environmental stress. With consistent use, it can soften the look
of dullness and post-blemish discoloration.

## How to use it

Many people use vitamin C in the morning before sunscreen. Start a few times a
week if you are new to it, and store unstable formulas away from heat and light.
