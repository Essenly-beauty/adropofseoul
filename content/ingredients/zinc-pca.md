## What it is

Zinc PCA combines zinc with PCA, a component related to the skin's natural
moisturizing factor. It is common in lightweight serums, toners, and gels for
oily or blemish-prone skin.

## What it does

It helps reduce the look and feel of excess shine while supporting a calmer
surface. It is not a full acne treatment by itself, but it can make a routine
feel more balanced.

## How to use it

Use it in the serum or moisturizer step, especially under sunscreen if your skin
gets shiny quickly. Pair with humectants so oil control does not turn into
dehydration.
