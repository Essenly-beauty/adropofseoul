## What it is

Azelaic acid is a multi-tasking ingredient used in both cosmetic and clinical
contexts. In skincare, lower-strength products often position it for redness,
blemishes, and uneven tone.

## What it does

It helps skin look calmer, less bumpy, and more even over time. It is especially
interesting for people who deal with both breakouts and lingering discoloration
after blemishes fade.

## How to use it

Start once daily or every other day, then build slowly. Use moisturizer to
buffer dryness and sunscreen to protect brightening progress.
