## What it is

Squalane is a stable, lightweight emollient related to squalene, a lipid found
in human sebum. Modern cosmetic squalane is typically plant-derived and used in
oils, creams, and softening serums.

## What it does

It smooths the skin surface, reduces the feeling of dryness, and helps a
moisturizer feel more flexible without becoming heavy. It is a nice bridge
between watery hydration and richer oils.

## How to use it

Use a few drops after serum or choose a moisturizer that already includes it.
It works well at night, in winter, or anytime your barrier feels dry but you do
not want a thick cream.
