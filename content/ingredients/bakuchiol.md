## What it is

Bakuchiol is a plant-derived active often discussed as a gentler alternative or
companion to retinol. It appears in creams and serums aimed at texture, glow,
and early signs of aging.

## What it does

It offers antioxidant support and can help skin look smoother and more even
with consistent use. Its main appeal is that it fits into well-aging routines
without the same reputation for peeling that retinol has.

## How to use it

Use it at night or morning depending on the formula. If you are sensitive, keep
the rest of the routine simple and pair it with moisturizer and sunscreen.
