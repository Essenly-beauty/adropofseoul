## What it is

Hyaluronic acid is a sugar-like molecule that can bind water at the skin's
surface. In Korean skincare it appears in toners, ampoules, sheet masks, and
gel creams because it gives a quick sense of bounce without adding oil.

## What it does

It pulls water into the upper layers of the skin, softening the look of fine
dehydration lines and helping makeup sit more smoothly. It is not a repair
ingredient on its own, but hydrated skin often feels calmer and more flexible.

## How to use it

Apply to slightly damp skin after cleansing, then follow with a cream or
lotion. It layers well with almost every active, including niacinamide,
centella, retinoids, and exfoliating acids.
