## What it is

Rice bran extract comes from the outer layer of rice grain. Rice water and rice
extract have a long place in East Asian beauty routines, and modern K-beauty
uses them in cleansers, toners, masks, and creams.

## What it does

It supports a softer, more luminous look rather than dramatic pigment fading.
Its appeal is gentle radiance: skin looks less dull, more comfortable, and a
little more polished over time.

## How to use it

Use it in a daily toner, essence, or cream. It pairs well with niacinamide,
panthenol, glycerin, and sunscreen in routines focused on glow.
