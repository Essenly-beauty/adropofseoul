## What it is

Tea tree leaf extract comes from Melaleuca alternifolia. It is not the same as
using undiluted tea tree essential oil; in skincare, it is usually folded into
toners, pads, gels, and spot-care products.

## What it does

It is used to make oily, blemish-prone skin feel cleaner and calmer. It will not
replace proven acne treatments for everyone, but it can be useful in a routine
that needs light sebum and redness support.

## How to use it

Use tea tree products selectively: after cleansing, on the T-zone, or on days
when skin feels congested. Balance it with soothing hydration so the routine
doesn't become stripping.
