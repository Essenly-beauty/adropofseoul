## What it is

Adenosine is a compound naturally involved in cellular energy signaling. In
Korean cosmetics it is a familiar wrinkle-care ingredient, often found in
creams, eye creams, and serums.

## What it does

It is used to support smoother-looking skin and soften the appearance of fine
lines over time. It is not an instant filler effect; think of it as a steady
well-aging support ingredient.

## How to use it

Use adenosine in a moisturizer, serum, or eye cream step. It layers easily with
panthenol, ceramides, peptides, niacinamide, and sunscreen.
