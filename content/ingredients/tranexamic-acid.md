## What it is

Tranexamic acid, often shortened to TXA, is a brightening ingredient that has
become more common in Korean serums and ampoules. It is especially popular in
formulas aimed at uneven tone that also want to stay gentle.

## What it does

It helps improve the look of dark patches, post-blemish marks, and dullness
over time. Unlike exfoliating acids, it is not mainly about peeling the surface;
it is more about supporting a calmer-looking pigment pathway.

## How to use it

Use once daily under moisturizer and sunscreen. It pairs well with niacinamide
and soothing ingredients, but go slowly if you also use retinoids or exfoliating
acids.
