## What it is

Mugwort extract is usually listed as artemisia extract on ingredient labels. In
Korean beauty culture, mugwort carries a long herbal tradition, and modern
skincare uses it in essences, wash-off masks, and calming creams.

## What it does

It is used to comfort skin that looks flushed or unsettled, especially when the
skin also feels oily or blemish-prone. Its appeal is the balance: calming
without the rich feel of many barrier creams.

## How to use it

Try it in watery layers after cleansing or as a soothing mask on days when skin
feels hot, congested, or stressed from weather and makeup.
