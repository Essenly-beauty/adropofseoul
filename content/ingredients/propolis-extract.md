## What it is

Propolis is a resin-like material bees use in the hive. In skincare, propolis
extract is used in ampoules, creams, and sleeping masks that aim for a soft,
healthy-looking glow.

## What it does

It offers antioxidant support and can make tired or dry skin look more supple.
Korean propolis products often combine it with honey, royal jelly, or humectants
for a cushiony finish.

## How to use it

Use it as a serum or moisturizer step when skin looks flat or feels
under-nourished. It is best for people who like a dewy, slightly comforting
finish rather than a completely matte one.
