## What it is

Madecassoside is one of the active compounds associated with centella asiatica.
While centella extract is a broad botanical ingredient, madecassoside is more
specific, which is why some Korean brands highlight it by name.

## What it does

It is used to calm the look of redness and support skin that feels overworked,
thin, or easily irritated. It also brings antioxidant support, making it a
useful companion to barrier-focused routines.

## How to use it

Look for it in cica creams, ampoules, masks, and post-active moisturizers. It
pairs well with panthenol, ceramides, glycerin, and low-strength niacinamide.
