## What it is

Allantoin is a calming, skin-conditioning ingredient used in products for
roughness, dryness, and irritation. It is common in Korean creams and toners
that promise comfort rather than dramatic overnight change.

## What it does

It helps skin feel smoother and less tight, especially when the surface is
chapped or stressed. It is not an exfoliating acid, but it can make rough skin
feel more refined by supporting a calmer surface.

## How to use it

Use it whenever your routine needs a softer landing: after cleansing, after
sunscreen removal, or alongside stronger actives that can make skin feel
sensitive.
