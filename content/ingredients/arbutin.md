## What it is

Arbutin is a brightening ingredient used in serums and creams for uneven tone.
Alpha-arbutin is a common form highlighted in products focused on dark spots or
post-acne marks.

## What it does

It helps reduce the look of excess pigment over time by targeting the pathway
involved in melanin production. It is usually gentler-feeling than strong acids,
which makes it useful in routines built for steady tone care.

## How to use it

Apply once daily at first, then increase if your skin tolerates it. Pair with
sunscreen every morning; brightening routines are never really complete without
UV protection.
