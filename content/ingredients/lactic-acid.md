## What it is

Lactic acid is an alpha hydroxy acid that exfoliates the skin surface. It is
also part of the skin's natural moisturizing factor, which is why lactic acid
products can feel less harsh than some other resurfacing formulas.

## What it does

It helps smooth flaky texture and brighten a dull-looking surface. Dry skin
often likes lactic acid because it can refine without the sharp feel of stronger
acid routines.

## How to use it

Use at night one to three times a week depending on strength. Keep the rest of
the routine moisturizing and avoid combining it with multiple other exfoliants.
