## What it is

Salicylic acid is a beta hydroxy acid, usually called BHA. Unlike water-loving
humectants, it is oil-soluble, which is why it is used in pore and blemish-care
products.

## What it does

It helps loosen dead skin and oil inside the pore, making it useful for
blackheads, clogged texture, and recurring breakouts. In K-beauty it often
appears in cleansers, toners, pads, and spot-care formulas.

## How to use it

Start once or twice a week in a leave-on product, or use a gentle BHA cleanser
if your skin is easily irritated. Follow with moisturizer and daily sunscreen.
