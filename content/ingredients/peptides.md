## What it is

Peptides are short chains of amino acids. Skincare uses many different types,
so "peptides" is more of a category than a single ingredient. Korean formulas
often place them in ampoules, creams, and eye products.

## What it does

Depending on the type, peptides may support a firmer look, smoother texture, or
better barrier comfort. They are popular because they fit into gentle routines
without the drama of stronger resurfacing actives.

## How to use it

Use peptide products consistently in the serum or moisturizer step. They pair
well with hydrating and barrier ingredients, and are easy to combine with
sunscreen-focused morning routines.
